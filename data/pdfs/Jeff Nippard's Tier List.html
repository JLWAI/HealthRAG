Here are the S tier and S+ tier exercises for muscle growth, grouped by body part focus, based on the information in the sources:

Glutes

  • S Tier:Machine hip abductions (also the number one exercise for the upper glutes)
  • Walking lunges
  • Elevated front foot lunges (on a small box)
  • 45° back extension


  • S+ Tier:Walking lunge (identified as the "best of the best")

Quads

  • S Tier:Barbell back squat
  • Hack squat
  • Pendulum squat
  • Smith machine squat
  • Bulgarian split squats


  • S+ Tier:Hack squat (identified as the "best of the best", with the high bar barbell back squat as an alternate go-to if a hack squat is unavailable)

Triceps

  • S Tier:Overhead cable triceps extension
  • Skull Crushers


  • S+ Tier:Overhead cable tricep extension (identified as the "best of the best")

Back

  • S Tier:Wide grip lat pull downs
  • Neutral grip lap pull downs
  • Half kneeling one arm lap pull Downs
  • Chest supported rows
  • Cable rows
  • Wide grip cable rows
  • Reverse pec deck (done sitting sideways)


  • S+ Tier:Chest supported row (identified as the "best of the best")

Biceps

  • S Tier:Preacher curl
  • Machine preacher curl
  • Face away Beian cable curl


  • S+ Tier:Face away Beian cable curl (identified as the "best of the best", with the 45° preacher curl as a close second)

Shoulders

  • S Tier:Cable lateral raise
  • Cable y raise
  • Behind the back cuffed lateral raise
  • Reverse cable crossover (identified as the number one rear delt builder)


  • S+ Tier:Cable lateral raise (identified as the "best of the best" for overall shoulder development)
  • The Machine shoulder press is also identified as the number one exercise for the front delts, and the Cable lateral raise is identified as the number one exercise for the side delts.
